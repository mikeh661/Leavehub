Thank you for your interest in this property. Your application will follow a three-stage process:

Stage 1 — Preliminary Questionnaire (this form)

We will review your responses and present them to the landlord for consideration. We aim to respond within 2–3 working days. You will be contacted directly with the outcome.

Stage 3 — Full Referencing
Should your application be successful and the landlord agree to proceed, you will be invited to undertake full formal referencing. At that stage, you will be required to pay a holding deposit in accordance with the Tenant Fees Act 2019.

Please be aware that if you are found to have provided false, misleading, or incomplete information within this application — and this subsequently results in a failed reference — your holding deposit may be retained by the agent to offset the reasonable costs incurred. This is in accordance with Schedule 2 of the Tenant Fees Act 2019.

We therefore ask that all information provided is accurate, truthful, and complete at the time of submission.
